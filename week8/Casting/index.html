Casting

Following up, I decided to cast the mould created during my experience with milling.

I chose to use transparent resin, but I ended up regreting tremendously. I had a hard time
removing the air bubbles and that caused the object to have a white/transparent color, which makes
it extremely difficult to visualize on the camera.

_____________________________________________________________________________

I poured both solutions A & B, on a ratio 1/1, inside of a cup and stired nonstop for around 3 minutes.

It took 4 hours for the resin to harden, but it eventually happened. I proceeded to remove it from the mould
and chop off the different objects. I covered the whole surface of the wax block, which means that not only my objects
were printed, but also an additional cool coin that was engraved in the block.

As a final reflection, I believe that being more careful during the mixing step would have solved the bubble problem.
I would also love to try different mixtures and colors if time was not a limitation.
