Tony Stark was my inspiration this time around, more specifically the technology he produces.</center> <center><img src="C:\Users\Felipe Augusto\Documents\GitHub\PHYS-S12\Images\Tony Stark.jpg" width="50%" height="50%"></center> <p>I first started with a self-destructive safe, but upon reflecting on the project and on my short term memory loss, I noticed that I was creating nothing more than a bomb (Just like Tony Stark). After this explosive idea, I moved on to think about a way to manipulate light and the idea of the hologram came along.</p> <p>My project is to create a prototype of a hologram, but not the boring pieces of plastic that sells on Amazon, an actual good one. A hologram that you can interact with is my idea. I find it particularly interesting because it allows you to enrich the space around you with thoughts, work, and entertainment.</p> <p>I plan for the design to be simple because the idea is already pretty defying. I want to create an open box with mirrors on the top and bottom and a black metal tube in the center to be the foundation of the projections.
