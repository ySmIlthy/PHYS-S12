Trying

3D Modeling

At first instance this second part of the assignment seemed the most overwhelming. However, I
had already worked with fusion360 in the past, which made this process 100% easier. The objects
selected by me were a single matchstick and a spray can.

The first step of this process was taking the measurements of both objects. This was not such an
influential thing for the matchstick due to its simplicity, yet for the spray can the narrow grooves
and the notches were really annoying to take measurements of, so those were estimated.

_____________________________________________________________________________

Matchstick

The matchstick consists of a rectangle with the sides, respectively, 2.2mm and 2.4mm.

_____________________________________________________________________________

Finish the sketch and extrude the rectangle 39.8mm up.

_____________________________________________________________________________

Create a sketch on the upper face of the stick and create a circle of 3.256mm of diameter.
Using the extrude tool, select the sphere option and create two spheres in the same spot as the circle.
The first one will be a joint and the other one will be a new body. The sphere should be 4mm.

_____________________________________________________________________________

Upon creating the two spheres, you will notice that the part of the sphere that goes inside
the stick has a line showing its boundaries. Filet this line on all 4 sides and you will have a
smoother matchstick. Same goes for the sides of the matchstick. The radius can be of choice, but
I prefer to do .5mm.

_____________________________________________________________________________

Spray Can

The core of the spray can consists of a circle of 66mm of diameter created on the sketch tool, extruded
up 160mm. Make sure that you make the center of the circle the center of the plane, it will come handy soon.

_____________________________________________________________________________

The core of the spray can consists of a circle of 66mm of diameter created on the sketch tool, extruded up 160mm.
Make sure that you make the center of the circle the center of the plane, it will come handy soon.

_____________________________________________________________________________

Create a sphere that fits the 2 circles.

_____________________________________________________________________________

After creating the sphere, go on the tab construct and select the option “offset plane”.
Select the circle laying down on the face of the cylinder and go 15mm up.

_____________________________________________________________________________

On this new plane, make 3 circles, respectively, 27mm, 30mm, and 35mm of diameter.
Extrude with the mode “cut”, 19mm towards the sphere. Proceed to extrude a new body, 9mm up on the
circle of 27mm of diameter and filet the top border line by 3mm. The same way, extrude, as joint, 4mm up
the area between the 30mm and 35mm circle and filet the under border line that touches the sphere by 11mm.
Concluding this first extrusion part, go back to the 2nd sketch and extrude 5mm deep the cylinder on the area
between the 56mm and 62mm circle.

_____________________________________________________________________________

Select the face on the center and create a 14mm circle on it and proceed to extrude it 10mm up. After creating
the projection, select the offset plane and select the face of the tip.

_____________________________________________________________________________

Create 4 circles on this new plane, respectively, with 1.5mm, 2mm, 5.5mm, and 6mm of diameter. Extrude cutting 1mm
of the area between the 1.5mm and 2mm circle, as well as the area between 5.5mm and 6mm, towards the tip.

_____________________________________________________________________________

From the top of the switch create a triangle that aligns with the center of the nozzle and extrude the semi-circle
that is opposite to the nozzle 2mm inwards and the triangle 1mm deep.

_____________________________________________________________________________

Filet 58mm of the line that separates the triangle from the semi-circle.
