Non-functional MUG

The assignment this time around consisted in creating an object with 2D cutting. When I first
started designing the shapes I was not sure of what I was going to build, but after printing
the pieces and moving them around the non-functional mug was born.

The process started with some circular shapes because my initial project was a ball, not a mug.
Pretty much all the work was made in fusion360, using the sketch tool.

You start selecting a plane to build your project on, it does not need to be a specific one.
Afterwards you select the circle option and create a circle with a diameter of 50mm.
Upon creating the circle you select from the same create tab, the point option and put
somewhere at the edge of the circle.

_____________________________________________________________________________

Use the circular pattern, on the create tab, to align the dot with four more of its kind.
Respectively selecting the dot on the edge as the object and the center of the circle as
the center point.

_____________________________________________________________________________

After creating the four points connect them using the line option, consequently forming a
shape similar to a rhombus. Upon completing the previous step you will want to filet the edges
of your shape using the filet tool. This can be achieved by selecting the filet tool on the
modify tab, and clicking on two adjacent lines of the rhombus, making the circumference 5mm.

_____________________________________________________________________________

At this point the surrounding circle can be deleted. From here you select the point created
behind the fileted edges and create a circle with a diameter of 3.8mm. After, you should
select the line and create a line from the lateral of the circle going to the outside of
the shape, creating a sort of rectangle with a round edge on each side.

_____________________________________________________________________________

Conclude trimming the rest of the shape making it look like the image below.

_____________________________________________________________________________

In this same sketch you are going to create the supports of the mug. Create on the side
of the completed shape a circle of 50mm and inside of the circle another one of 25mm.
Upon creating the circles make a straight line crossing both circles vertically, leaving
you with two equal sides.

_____________________________________________________________________________

Trim the 50mm side on the right and the line inside of the 25mm circle.

_____________________________________________________________________________

Create lines at the middle of the surviving vertical lines, expanding 5mm on the left side.
From the created lines pick their middle and connect both top and bottom. After, you should
trim the 25mm on the right side where the line that goes from top and bottom crosses.

_____________________________________________________________________________

At this point you can get rid of the line that touches the 5mm lines. Create circles of 3.8mm
at the tip of the 5mm lines and expand lines from the sides of the just created circles going
to the outside similar to the previous shape created.

_____________________________________________________________________________

Filet as shown below and finish sketch.

_____________________________________________________________________________

Then go to the sketch folder on the left side and save as DXF. The archive should look
like the image below.

_____________________________________________________________________________

Afterwards, you should do the laser cut of 2 rhombus and 5 semi-circles.

_____________________________________________________________________________

Assemble 4 semi circles with 2 rhombus and use the
last semi-circle to be the handle of the object.
